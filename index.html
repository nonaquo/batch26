FROM YOUR ADVISERS

A Note From
Your Teachers.

Words written with love — from the ones who watched you grow every single day.

PATRIARCHS
AP
Ma'am Aizel Parale
Class Adviser — Patriarchs
"My Dear Patriarchs, Hello, my panganays! Congratulations! malapit na ang inyong most-awaited day, your Graduation. While I know you’re all excited, there’s also a part of me that feels a little heavy knowing that things won’t be the same anymore. I won’t be able to enter Room 302 the way I used to from July 14 until April. I won’t be getting the same feeling of warmth and laughter shared with you all kasi wala na kayo here. All of you made my first experience truly unforgettablesince kayo ang nagparamdam sakin how I truly belong in this profession. MY first panganays, MY sandalan, MY comfort, MY babies! I will truly miss all of you more than words can express. I will never forget the moment you introduced me to the “Patriarchs” during Zhen’s birthday. It still makes me smile whenever I remember it. That was also the moment I felt a sense of relief. I was so nervous and anxious handling all of you because you are all amazing and brilliant students. But that day, you made me feel like I truly belong, that I was meant to meet you, my Patriarchs. Wahhhh this is not so meee.Hindi talaga ako ganito ka-emotional, but here I am, feeling both proud and sad at the same time. Proud because I know you will all go on to achieve great things, and sad because you will now take different paths, go to different schools, and chase your dreams in your chosen fields.. Thank you for welcoming me, Thank you for being my PAHINGA. Kahit ang kukulit nyo minsan. I will miss all the memories we shared together, the accomplishments na nareceived nyo and the warmth of love you gave so freely. Wahhh hindi talaga ako tooo. Wait naiiyak na ako buti letter to HAHAAHHA. Don’t forget meeeee haaaa. I can’t think of anything pa na sabihin sa letter na to kundi thank you thank you thank you talaga sa inyooo. I don’t know if I would want to handle any section pa next year kasi baka maalala kolang kayo everytime huhu. Before I started teaching, I wasn’t really sure of myself, It’s like something was missing. But looking back sa memories natin together as section, I finally realized that I was ready back then, that missing piece ay kayo. You made me complete, All of you helped me see things more clearly. All of you became a big part of my growth not just as a teacher but also as a person. I am and will always be proud of you all, My Patriarch babies. Walang time na binigo nyo ako. Thank you very much for being responsible, for doing your best, and for making me proud every single day. I-end ko na to kasi humahaba na yan sya HAAHAHAH. Good luck on your journey, always remember that Ma’am AIZ Loves you alll. Mahal na mahal ko kayo!!! Love, Ma’am Aiz"
✦
HEAVEN
LO
Ma'am Lealyn Ocampo
Class Adviser — Heaven
" Dear My Future Doctors, Sa totoo lamang, hindi ko alam kung paano o saan ko sisimulan ang mensaheng ito para sa inyo. Sa walong buwan na tayo’y magkasama, nasaksihan ko hindi lamang ang inyong sipag sa pag-aaral, kundi pati na rin ang inyong tawanan, kulitan, iyakan, at higit sa lahat—ang inyong katatagan sa bawat hamon na inyong hinarap. Every moment we shared has truly meant so much to me. Palagi ninyo akong tinatanong, “Ma’am Lea, mamimiss niyo po ba kami?” Madalas ko mang sabihin na hindi, pero ang totoo… sobra-sobra ko kayong mamimiss. Hindi ninyo alam kung gaano kalaki ang naging bahagi ninyo sa puso ko. Napakarami ko ring natutuhan mula sa inyo, mga anak. Mahal na mahal ko kayo. At ngayon, nakikita kong handa na kayong lahat para sa susunod na yugto ng inyong buhay—ang pagpasok sa kolehiyo. Huwag na huwag ninyong kakalimutan na proud na proud ako sa inyong lahat—hindi lamang sa kung ano ang narating ninyo, kundi sa kung sino kayo bilang mga tao. Dalangin ko ang inyong tagumpay, kalakasan, at patuloy na gabay sa bawat hakbang na tatahakin ninyo. Nawa’y manatili kayong mabubuting tao, may malasakit, at may paninindigan sa buhay. Kasama kayong lahat sa aking mga panalangin, saan man kayo dalhin ng inyong mga pangarap. Sa aking mga Future Doctors, Padayon at Pagpalain kayo! With all my love, Ma’am Lea"

PROPHETS
HC
Ma'am Hazel CarolinO
Class Adviser — Prophets
"Sa aking minamahal na Grade 12 – Queen of Prophets, Kayo ang aking unang advisory class, kaya naman napakaespesyal ninyo sa aking puso. Habang papalapit na ang inyong pagtatapos, nais kong ipabatid kung gaano ako kaproud sa bawat isa sa inyo. Alam kong hindi naging madali ang ating mga pinagdaanan, may mga pagsubok, pagod, at sakripisyo, ngunit napatunayan ninyo na kaya ninyong lampasan ang lahat ng iyon. Hinding-hindi ko makakalimutan ang mga alaala nating nabuo, mga bunso ang ating mga tawanan, kwentuhan, at maging ang mga hamon na sabay-sabay nating hinarap. Ipinaranas ninyo sa akin ang lahat ng aking mga “first time” bilang inyong gurong tagapayo, at habang-buhay ko itong pahahalagahan. Maraming salamat sa tiwala, pagmamahal, at respeto na ibinigay ninyo sa akin. Mahal na mahal ko kayo, at lagi kong ipagmamalaki kung sino man ang inyong mararating sa hinaharap. Dalhin ninyo ang mga aral, alaala, at pagkakaibigang nabuo ninyo sa taong ito. Huwag kayong matakot mangarap at sumubok, dahil ang bawat hakbang ninyo ay magdadala sa inyo sa mas maliwanag at magandang kinabukasan. Nawa’y manatili kayong mabuti, matatag, at may malasakit sa kapwa saan man kayo dalhin ng inyong mga pangarap. Lagi ninyong tandaan na naniniwala ako sa kakayahan ninyo. Ngayon pa lamang ay binabati ko na kayo, mga bunso, my dear Queen of Prophets. I am forever proud of you. Nagmamahal, Bb. Hazel"
